Saanp aur Sanpera

साँप और संपेरा

Manoj Comics › Misc

A vintage Misc issue in Hindi by Manoj Comics that was published in 1980s (not sure when). The story features how a devil snake (naag) got unleashed and created disturbances and destruction everywhere.

It's purely a part of fiction written by Asheet Chatterjee and the fantastic art segment done by Dileep Kadam and team. Enjoy reading :).
