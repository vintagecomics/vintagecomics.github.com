Grihasth Vetal

ग्ृहस्थ वेताल

Indrajal Comics › Vetal

A rare vintage issue published in 1976 by Times of India publication for Indrajal Comics featuring Vetal, the walking ghost, in a dilemma to leave the jungle, his culture and tradition to marry Diana Palmer or not. He faces the city life for the first time as a normal person and tries to make a living there.

The Ghost has given a try to the modern lifestyle for a while for his love. Read on to enjoy the full story.
