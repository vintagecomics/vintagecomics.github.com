Vetal or the Phantom or the Ghost who walks was the most popular comic book character in the era of 1960s to 1980s in India. The character carries a manly, heroic trait and his pledge against the sea pirates makes him connected to the real world in some ways.

The character of Vetal is powerful, wild, and adventurous - he follows the tradition started by his forefathers to become Vetal to fight evil and cessar sea pirates.
