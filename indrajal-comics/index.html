Indrajal Comics is one of the oldest names in Indian comic book industry. The series was started by Times of India, and Bennet-Coleman & Co. in 1960s with issues published in several regional languages.

The comic series is mostly popular due to the characters like The Phantom, Mandrake (both by Lee Falk, Flash Gordon, Buz Sawyer and more. The series ended in 1989 by the decision of the company.

Enjoy below the vintage issues published by Indrajal Comics in it's 27-year long life span.
